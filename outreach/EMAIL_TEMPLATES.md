## Phone Script

**Opening:**
"Hi, is this {first_name}? This is {sender_name} from Contractor Verified ATX. I'm not selling anything — I actually run Austin's largest contractor community, and I'm looking to add a few quality {trade} contractors to our verified network. You've got great reviews, and I wanted to see if you'd be interested in getting featured to our 63,000 members. Do you have 2 minutes?"

**If yes:**
"Great. So Contractor Verified ATX is a directory and Facebook community of homeowners looking for trusted contractors. We verify contractors — check reviews, licenses, insurance — then give them a verified badge and feature them to our members. You'd get your own profile page, priority leads, and we post about verified contractors in the group. It's free to join. All I need is a few details. Ready?"

**Close:**
"Perfect. I'll send you an email with next steps. You'll need to upload your insurance cert and license. Once verified, you'll be live on the site and featured in the group. Any questions?"

## Campaign Timeline

**Day 1:** Send initial email
**Day 3:** Follow-up email if no response
**Day 5:** SMS if still no response  
**Day 7:** Final email
**Day 10:** Move to "unresponsive" if no reply

---

## Tracking Fields

- `status`: new → contacted → interested → signed_up → declined → unresponsive
- `outreach_count`: increment on each touch
- `last_contacted_at`: timestamp of last outreach
- `notes`: capture responses, objections, next steps
